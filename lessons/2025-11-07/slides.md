# Introduction

<div class="text-center space-y-6">

## Historical Context

<div class="bg-purple-50 p-8 rounded-lg text-left max-w-4xl mx-auto space-y-4">

Joseph Smith wrote letters to the Saints about the **doctrine of baptism for the dead**.

He expounded upon biblical prophecies to help the Saints understand the importance of being baptized on behalf of their relatives who had died.

# Background Context

<div class="text-center space-y-6">

## Joseph Smith's Situation

<div class="text-left max-w-4xl mx-auto space-y-6">

### The Accusation
Missouri authorities accused Joseph Smith of helping plan an attack on former governor Lilburn W. Boggs.

### The Response
Joseph suspected if he returned to Missouri, he would be killed, so he went into hiding.

### The Revelation
While hiding in Edward Hunter's home, Joseph had many thoughts and feelings about baptisms for the dead.

<div class="text-center mt-6 text-lg italic text-blue-600">
See [Doctrine and Covenants 128:1](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/128.1?lang=eng#p1)
</div>

</div>

</div>

<!--
Presenter Notes:
- Historical context is important for understanding these revelations

- The persecution didn't stop the work - if anything, it accelerated revelation
- Even in hiding, he was thinking about the welfare of the Saints
- Reference: Saints: The Story of the Church, vol. 1, pages 466-77

# Question 1: Dispensation of the Fulness of Times

<div class="space-y-6">

## Definition

<div class="p-6 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg">

**Dispensation:** A period of time when the Lord reveals His doctrines, ordinances, and priesthood.

**The Dispensation of the Fulness of Times:** The current dispensation that began with Joseph Smith.

</div>

## Joseph Smith Taught

<div class="p-6 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg text-lg">

> "The dispensation of the fullness of times will bring to light the things that have been revealed in all former dispensations; also other things that have not been before revealed. He shall send Elijah, the Prophet, etc., and restore all things in Christ"

<div class="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-4">
Teachings of Presidents of the Church: Joseph Smith, pages 510-11
</div>

</div>

</div>

<!--
Presenter Notes:
- This is THE final dispensation before Christ's Second Coming
- All previous truths are being restored
- New things are also being revealed
- Elijah's mission (turning hearts to fathers) is key to this dispensation

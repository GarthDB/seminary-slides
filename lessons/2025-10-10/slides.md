# God's Perspective vs. Our Perspective

::left::

## Our View
*"Why isn't this working?"*

- We see immediate problems
- We want quick fixes
- We focus on temporal needs
- We measure success our way

**Human Timeline:** NOW

::right::

## God's View
*"I have much treasure in this city"*

- God sees eternal purposes
- He prepares us for greater things
- He values spiritual growth
- His success looks different

**God's Timeline:** ETERNAL

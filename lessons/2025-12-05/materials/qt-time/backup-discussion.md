# Backup Discussion Questions

If you need additional discussion topics or if students finish early, here are some thought-provoking questions related to today's lesson:

## Questions About D&C 137

1. **Why do you think the Lord showed Joseph Smith this vision at this particular time?**
   - Consider: Joseph was in the Kirtland Temple, preparing for its dedication
   - He had carried questions about Alvin for 13 years

2. **How might Joseph's personal losses (Alvin, his children) have prepared him to receive this revelation?**
   - Sometimes our trials prepare us to receive answers
   - Our experiences help us understand doctrine more deeply

3. **What false traditions or beliefs about heaven have you heard from other faiths or sources?**
   - How does D&C 137 correct these misunderstandings?
   - Why is it important to know the truth about who can be saved?

## Personal Application Questions

4. **Do you have any righteous desires that haven't been fulfilled yet?**
   - How does D&C 137:9 give you hope?
   - What does it mean to you that God knows the desires of your heart?

5. **Have you had experiences with family history work?**
   - How did it make you feel?
   - Did you learn anything surprising about your ancestors?

6. **Why do you think temple work is so important if people will be judged by "the desires of their hearts"?**
   - Consider: Ordinances are still required for exaltation
   - We perform ordinances for those who would have accepted them

## Deep Thinking Questions

7. **How does knowing that children who die before age 8 are saved in the celestial kingdom help you understand God's character?**
   - What does this teach us about God's mercy?
   - What does this teach us about the Atonement of Jesus Christ?

8. **If someone "would have received" the gospel, what do you think that means?**
   - How does God know what someone would have done?
   - What does this teach us about God's knowledge and judgment?

9. **How might the world be different if everyone understood the truths taught in D&C 137?**
   - Would people treat each other differently?
   - Would people be more motivated to do family history work?

## Scenario Discussion

10. **Imagine you're talking to a friend whose baby sibling just passed away. The friend is worried about whether the baby can go to heaven. What would you say?**
    - What scriptures would you share?
    - How would you help them feel comfort?

11. **You have a friend who is Catholic, and they're worried because their grandparent died without being baptized in the "right" church. What would you share from D&C 137?**
    - How can you be sensitive to their beliefs while sharing truth?
    - What hope can you offer?

## Testimony Building

12. **How has learning about these truths strengthened your testimony of:**
    - The Restoration of the gospel through Joseph Smith?
    - The Atonement of Jesus Christ?
    - Heavenly Father's plan of salvation?
    - The importance of temple and family history work?

13. **What will you do differently this week because of what you learned today?**

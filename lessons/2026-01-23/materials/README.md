# Lesson Materials - January 23, 2026

## Lesson: Moses 4:5–32; 5:1–15, Part 1—"The Eyes of Them Both Were Opened"

### Source
- [Old Testament Seminary Student Manual (2026) - Lesson 11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/manual/old-testament-seminary-student-manual-2026/04-genesis-3-4-moses-4-5/042-moses-4-5-32-5-1-15-part-1?lang=eng)

### Key Scriptures
- Moses 4:6–13, 22–25; 5:10–11
- Moses 4:13–16, 22–25, 28–29; 5:1–4
- 2 Nephi 2:22–25

### Lesson Purpose
To help students understand the effects of the Fall in Heavenly Father's plan and in their own lives.

### Key Points
- The Fall was essential to Heavenly Father's plan
- Without the Fall, no mortal children would have been born
- The Fall made mortality, opposition, and growth possible
- The Fall is necessary for eternal life

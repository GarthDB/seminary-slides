# Lesson Materials - November 14, 2025

**Friday, November 14, 2025 - Last Day of Class**

## Overview

This is the last day of class for the trimester. The lesson focuses on **Doctrinal Mastery Practice 9** with an emphasis on reflection and celebration of what students learned during the trimester.

## Files in This Directory

- **lesson.md** - Full lesson plan with all Canvas lesson content, discussion questions, and activities
- **qt-time/jamie.md** - QT Time question from Jamie: "What did you learn this trimester?"

## Key Topics

1. **Doctrinal Mastery Review**
   - Three principles of acquiring spiritual knowledge
   - Key passages from D&C 130-132
   
2. **Trimester Reflection**
   - What students learned
   - How they've grown
   - Commitments going forward

3. **This Week's Lessons**
   - D&C 130 - Principles of truth
   - D&C 131 - Celestial marriage
   - D&C 132:1-2, 34-66 - New and everlasting covenant

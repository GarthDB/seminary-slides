# Three Prophets, Three Keys

::left::

## 1. Moses - Gathering Israel
*"The keys of the gathering of Israel"*

**[D&C 110:11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/110.11?lang=eng)**

- Missionary work worldwide
- Building faith and testimony
- Preaching the restored gospel
- Gathering scattered Israel

::right::

## 2. Elias - Gospel of Abraham
*"The dispensation of the gospel of Abraham"*

**[D&C 110:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/110.12?lang=eng)**

- Abrahamic covenant restored
- All nations blessed through gospel
- Priesthood blessings available
- Right to eternal life

<!--
Presenter Notes:
Moses - Keys of Gathering Israel ([D&C 110:11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/110.11?lang=eng))
- Full quote: "The keys of the gathering of Israel from the four parts of the earth"
- This includes missionary work, temple work, and family history work
- Students can participate by sharing the gospel and building faith in others
- President Nelson: "We are referring to missionary, temple, and family history work"

Elias - Dispensation of Abraham ([D&C 110:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/110.12?lang=eng))
- Full quote: "The dispensation of the gospel of Abraham, saying that in us and our seed all generations after us should be blessed"
- The Abrahamic covenant includes priesthood, gospel, and eternal life
- Students have access to these same covenant blessings
- All nations blessed through the restored gospel and priesthood
-->

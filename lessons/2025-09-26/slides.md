Historical Context - March 28, 1835:

- The Lord revealed D&C 107 to instruct them about priesthood authority and encourage them

- Application: We need to stay connected to Christ to have spiritual power in our service
- This connects to today's lesson about priesthood authority coming from Christ

## 1. Christ is the Source

## 2. All Callings Connected  
*"All other authorities... are appendages to this priesthood"*

**[D&C 107:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p5#p5)**

<!--
Presenter Notes:
Principle 1: Christ is the Source ([D&C 107:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p3#p3))
- Full quote: "The Holy Priesthood, after the Order of the Son of God"
- All priesthood authority comes from Jesus Christ
- It is His work, in which we are privileged to assist
- Connection to the Savior is essential for spiritual power
- President Uchtdorf taught: "Christ is the source of all true priesthood authority and power on earth."

Principle 2: All Callings are Connected ([D&C 107:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p5#p5))
- Full quote: "All other authorities or offices in the church are appendages to this priesthood"
- Every Church calling draws from priesthood authority
- Relief Society, Young Women, Primary - all connected to the Melchizedek Priesthood
- Both men and women exercise priesthood authority in their callings
- [D&C 107:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p8#p8): The Melchizedek Priesthood "has power and authority... to administer in spiritual things"

# Discussion

<div class="space-y-6 text-left max-w-2xl mx-auto text-lg">

**Why does it matter that priesthood power comes from Christ?**

**How do ordinances connect us to the Savior?**

**How can this help you serve better?**

</div>

<!--
Presenter Notes:
Discussion Questions (expand on these as needed):

1. Why is it important to understand that all priesthood power comes directly from Jesus Christ?
   - Helps us remember we're serving Him, not ourselves
   - Gives us confidence in our authority when we serve
   - Reminds us to seek His guidance and power

2. How do the "outward ordinances" of the Aaronic Priesthood (like baptism and the sacrament) help connect you to Jesus Christ?
   - Baptism symbolizes rebirth and commitment to follow Christ
   - Sacrament renews our covenants and remembrance of Him
   - These ordinances are physical reminders of spiritual truths

3. What can we learn from comparing Church organizations to "appendages" of the Melchizedek Priesthood?
   - All are connected to the same source of authority
   - Each has a specific function but all work together
   - Young women, Primary, Relief Society all derive authority from Christ

4. How might understanding that every calling is attached to the Savior's authority help you better serve others?
   - Gives confidence to act in your calling
   - Reminds you that you represent Christ
   - Helps you seek His power to bless others
-->

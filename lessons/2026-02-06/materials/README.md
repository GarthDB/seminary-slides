# Moses 7:53-69 Lesson Materials

**Date:** February 6, 2026  
**Lesson:** Moses 7:53-69 - "The King of Zion Will Preserve His People"  
**Time:** 40 minutes (12:00-12:40 PM)

## Lesson Overview

This lesson focuses on the Savior as the King of Zion and how He preserves His people in the last days. The main theme is **seeing and appreciating what the Lord is doing to help us overcome the trials of our time**.

### Key Learning Objective

Students will **see and appreciate what the Lord is doing to help them overcome the trials of our time**.

## Lesson Structure

### Opening (7-10 minutes)
- **Prayer** (2 min)
- **QT Time with Gabe** (5-8 min)
  - Question: "When have you felt the Lord helping you through a hard time or trial?"
  - Backup question available in `qt-time/backup-discussion.md`

### Main Lesson (~25-30 minutes)

#### 1. Prophesied Difficulties (5-7 min)
- Study D&C 45:26-28, 31, 33; Moses 7:60-61, 66
- President Nelson's perspective on the Second Coming

#### 2. The King of Zion (5-7 min)
- Moses 7:53 - titles the Savior used to describe Himself
- Enoch's vision and the Lord's comfort

#### 3. How the Lord Preserves His People (10-12 min)
- Moses 7:60-62 - what the Lord promised to do
- Three ways: righteousness from heaven, truth from the earth, gather to Zion
- Optional: Options A, B, or C for deeper study

#### 4. Personal Application (5-7 min)
- How is God preserving you?
- Personal reflection

### Closing (3-5 minutes)
- Weekly challenge
- Closing prayer

## Main Teaching Points

1. **The Savior is "the King of Zion"** (Moses 7:53) - He watches over and preserves His people.

2. **In the last days** the Lord will send righteousness from heaven, send truth from the earth, and gather His people to Zion (Moses 7:62).

3. **We will see the greatest manifestations of the Savior's power** that the world has ever seen (President Nelson).

4. **The Lord comforts and teaches** those who seek Him, as He did Enoch.

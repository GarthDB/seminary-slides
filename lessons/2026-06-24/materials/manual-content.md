# Manual content outline (2026-06-24)

- **Page title:** Lesson 78: 1 Kings 3—The Lord Blesses Us to Accomplish His Work
- **Lesson label:** 1 Kings 3: Lesson 78
- **Scripture block:** 1 Kings 3
- **Subtitle:** The Lord Blesses Us to Accomplish His Work

## Purpose

Help students understand that the Lord will bless them with the ability to accomplish His work.

## Principle

As we humbly recognize our weakness and seek the Lord's help to bless others, He will increase our ability to accomplish His work.

## Core scripture passages

- 1 Kings 1:11–13 — Solomon chosen as king
- 1 Kings 3:3 — Solomon's desire to be righteous
- 1 Kings 3:5 — The Lord appears and says "Ask what I shall give thee"
- 1 Kings 3:6–9 — Solomon's humble request: "Give thy servant an understanding heart"
- 1 Kings 3:10–14 — The Lord's response: wisdom, riches, and honour

## Supporting examples

Students read one example of Solomon's righteous use of blessings:

- 1 Kings 3:16–28 — Dispute between two women (the living child)
- 1 Kings 4:24–25, 29–32, 34 — Fruits of Solomon's reign

## Church leader quotes

**President Thomas S. Monson:**
"When on the Lord's errand, members are entitled to divine support. The Lord will strengthen individuals for their assignments."

**Elder Larry R. Lawrence:**
Invites students to spiritually ask: "What is keeping me from progressing?" and await divine response for personal revelation.

**Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf:**
"God will magnify talents and abilities of those offering willing hearts, making them perfect through Christ without requiring flawlessness."

## Study options

### Option A: Recognizing Weaknesses
- **Question:** How could recognizing my weaknesses prepare me to do the Lord's work?
- **Scriptures:** Ether 12:27; D&C 112:10
- **Activity:** Complete Elder Lawrence's spiritual exercise; record impressions about acting in faith to overcome weaknesses

### Option B: Accessing God's Power
- **Question:** How can I access God's power to increase my ability to accomplish His work?
- **Scriptures:** Helaman 12:1; D&C 19:38; D&C 78:17–19; D&C 64:31–34; Moroni 10:32–33
- **Activity:** Add to the bridge drawing (gap between "The Lord's Work" and personal limitations) blessings that could help participate in the Lord's work, plus personal examples of divine magnification

## Closing discussion prompts

- Share their created drawings
- Personal testimony about the Lord's ability to help accomplish His work
- Specific actions taken toward the lesson purpose
- Questions raised and methods for finding answers

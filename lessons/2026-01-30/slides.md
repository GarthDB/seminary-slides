# The Doctrine of Christ: Four Steps

<div class="space-y-3">

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-sm">

<v-clicks>

<div class="p-4 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg">

**1. Believe in Him**

Trust that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and our Savior

</div>

<div class="p-4 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">

**2. Repent**

Turn away from sin and turn toward God

</div>

<div class="p-4 bg-purple-50 rounded-lg">

**3. Be Baptized**

Make covenants with God through baptism

</div>

<div class="p-4 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg">

Be confirmed and receive the constant companionship of the Holy Ghost

# Sacred, confidential, personal

<div class="space-y-3 text-sm text-left max-w-4xl mx-auto">

<div class="p-4 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border border-red-100">

**From the General Handbook (18.17.1)** — help the class understand **why** we protect patriarchal blessings:

- Each blessing is **sacred, confidential, and personal** (given in private).
- **Treasure** the words; **ponder** them; live worthy of promised blessings.
- **Do not compare** blessings; **do not share** except with **close family**; **do not read** in Church meetings or public gatherings.

</div>

# Lineage in Israel

::left::

## Who we are

Patriarchal blessings declare **lineage** (literal or by adoption) in the **house of Israel** and the family of **Abraham**.

::right::

## Abrahamic covenant

God’s covenant with Abraham was renewed with **Isaac** and **Jacob** (Israel). All tribes can receive the covenant’s blessings and responsibilities.

<div class="text-xs mt-4">See <a href="https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/26?lang=eng&id=p3-p4#p3">Genesis 26:3–4</a> and <a href="https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/28?lang=eng&id=p12-p15#p12">Genesis 28:12–15</a></div>

# A Liahona of light

<div class="space-y-6 max-w-3xl mx-auto">

<div class="p-8 bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-50 to-purple-50 rounded-xl italic text-lg leading-relaxed">

"Your patriarchal blessing is **yours and yours alone.** It may be brief or lengthy, simple or profound. Length and language do not a patriarchal blessing make. It is the **Spirit** that conveys the true meaning. Your blessing is not to be folded neatly and tucked away. It is **not to be framed or published.** Rather, it is to be **read.** It is to be **loved.** It is to be **followed.**"

<div class="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-4 not-italic">President Thomas S. Monson, "Your Patriarchal Blessing: A Liahona of Light," Ensign, Nov. 1986</div>

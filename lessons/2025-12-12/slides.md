# Background Context

<div class="space-y-3">

## What Was the Policy?

<div class="p-4 bg-gray-50 rounded-lg text-sm">

From the introduction to [Official Declaration 2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/od/2?lang=eng):

"Early in its history, Church leaders stopped conferring the priesthood on Black males of African descent. Church records offer no clear insights into the origins of this practice."

</div>

## Important Truths

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg">

**From the Start:**

[2 Nephi 26:33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/2-ne/26.33?lang=eng) teaches that God invites "all to come unto him... black and white, bond and free, male and female."

</div>

<div class="p-3 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">

**Church Position:**

We do not know why this restriction was in place. We should not speculate about the reasons.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<!--
Presenter Notes:
- The introduction to OD 2 says "Church records offer no clear insights into the origins"
- This is the official position of the Church
- We don't know why it was in place
- But we do know God loves all His children equally
- 2 Nephi 26:33 has always been clear that all are invited to come unto Christ
- From the beginning, Church leaders prayed for the day when all could hold the priesthood
-->
